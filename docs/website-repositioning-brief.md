# IndusAI Website Repositioning Brief

Generated on 2026-04-11 from the current site structure and the new positioning direction.

## Objective

Reposition the website from:

- a voice AI solutions/services company

to:

- an enterprise agentic AI product company
- with a multi-product suite
- with platform capabilities
- with enterprise deployment credibility

## Core Brand Shift

### Old Perception

- Voice AI company
- Multilingual support provider
- Industry solutions vendor
- Services-led / solutions-led narrative

### New Perception

- Agentic AI product company
- Enterprise SaaS / product-led company
- Multiple products with distinct use cases
- Platform + products + industry deployments

## Recommended Brand Framing

### Master Brand

Use `IndusAI` as the parent/master brand unless the business intends to fully rebrand at the company level.

### Product Names in UI

Preferred display format:

- `IndusLabs`
- `FinoLabs`
- `Agentic AI SM`

Optional secondary label:

- `induslabs.io`
- `finolabs.io`

Do not use raw domains as the primary product labels in the main UI.

## Positioning Statement

### Recommended One-Line Positioning

`IndusAI is an enterprise agentic AI product company building products for voice, workflows, financial operations, and autonomous business automation.`

### Shorter Version

`The agentic AI product suite for modern enterprises.`

### Internal Strategic Summary

Move Indus from a `voice AI solutions provider` to an `enterprise agentic AI product company with a multi-product suite`.

## Homepage Strategy

### Current Problem

The current homepage leads with a voice AI capability and then moves quickly into industries. That makes the site feel like a services/solutions company instead of a product company.

### Recommended Homepage Flow

1. Hero
2. Products
3. Platform Capabilities
4. Industry / Functional Use Cases
5. Outcomes / Proof
6. Enterprise Trust
7. Final CTA

## Hero Repositioning

This is too narrow and voice-specific.

### Recommended Hero Directions

Option 1:

`Enterprise Agentic AI Products Built for Real Business Workflows`

Option 2:

`The Agentic AI Product Suite for Modern Enterprises`

Option 3:

`From Voice Agents to Autonomous Workflows — AI Products Built for Scale`

### Recommended Hero Copy

Primary line:

`IndusAI builds agentic AI products that help enterprises automate conversations, workflows, and decisions across customer support, finance, operations, and growth.`

Secondary line:

`Deploy voice agents, financial workflow automation, and autonomous enterprise systems from a single AI product ecosystem.`

### Recommended Hero CTAs

- `Explore Products`
- `Book a Demo`

- `Talk to Sales`

### Hero Proof / Sub-messaging

Instead of only highlighting multilingual support, emphasize:

- Voice agents
- Workflow automation
- Domain-specific products
- Enterprise deployment
- Measurable business outcomes

## Use Cases / Solutions Framing

### Current Problem

The site currently leans too heavily on industry-first messaging, which makes the company feel services-led.

### Recommended Change

Industries should support the product story, not replace it.

The use-case layer should answer:

- Which products fit which functions?
- Which workflows can be automated?
- Which industries are strongest initial fits?

### Recommended Use Case Categories

- Customer Support
- Sales and Lead Qualification
- Lending and Collections
- Financial Servicing
- Booking and Scheduling
- Enterprise Operations
- Internal Workflow Automation

### Industry Layer

Use industries as secondary proof:

- Banking & Finance
- Hospitality
- Healthcare
- Retail / Commerce
- Automotive
- Enterprise Operations

## Outcomes / Proof Section

### Purpose

Make the site feel real and productized, not conceptual.

### Recommended Content Types

- Outcome metrics
- Customer logos
- Product-specific case snippets
- Deployment numbers
- Automation metrics

### Suggested Metrics Direction

- Reduction in handling time
- Increase in automation rate
- Faster collections or servicing outcomes
- Multilingual support coverage
- Speed of deployment

## Enterprise Trust Section

### Purpose

Close the gap between product marketing and enterprise procurement confidence.

### Recommended Trust Themes

- Security and compliance
- Deployment flexibility
- Integrations
- Observability and governance
- Human control and escalation

### Suggested Heading

`Built for Enterprise Deployment`

### Suggested Supporting Points

- Secure deployment architecture
- Integrates with existing systems
- Human-in-the-loop controls
- Monitoring and analytics
- Ready for regulated environments

### Products Page

Current issue:

- Reads like technical capability inventory, not a real product page

New goal:

- Explain the three products
- Clarify target buyers/users
- Show workflows each product solves
- Explain deployment and integrations
- Show how products relate to the core platform

Recommended structure:

1. Products hero
2. Three product detail blocks
3. Shared platform capabilities
4. Deployment and integration section
5. Product-to-use-case mapping
6. CTA

### Solutions Page

Current issue:

New goal:

Recommended structure:

1. Functional use cases
2. Industry deployments
3. Product mapping
4. Enterprise outcomes

### About Page

Current issue:

- Generic AI company language

New goal:

- Emphasize mission around agentic systems

- Building enterprise-grade agentic products
- Focused on production deployment, not demos alone
- Creating practical systems for real business workflows

## Working Assumptions for Initial Copy

These assumptions can be used to proceed before leadership finalizes all naming/messaging details:

- Parent brand remains `IndusAI`
- `IndusLabs` is the flagship voice + conversational AI product
- `FinoLabs` is BFSI-first and finance-operations oriented
- `Agentic AI SM` refers to a broader autonomous agent system / multi-agent platform
- Desired tone is enterprise-first, product-led, modern SaaS, not consulting-led

## Open Questions

These should be confirmed before final production copy:

1. What does `Agentic AI SM` stand for exactly?
2. Is `FinoLabs` strictly BFSI-first, or should it be positioned more broadly across finance and operations?
3. Should the parent brand on the site remain `IndusAI`, or shift toward `IndusLabs` / `Indus Labs`?
4. Should the visual tone land closer to classic enterprise/corporate, or modern premium SaaS?
